Polytope

In CORA, polytopes are instantiated by
P = polytope(A,b);
where A is the inequality constraint matrix, b is the inequality offset vector, and in vertex representation by
P = polytope(V);
where V is a point cloud.
Example:
% initialize polytope
P = polytope([1 1; -1 1; 0 -1],[1;1;1]);
% plot polytope
plot(P);
